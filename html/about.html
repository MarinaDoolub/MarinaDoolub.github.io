About Me


I began my journey studying science with the intention of becoming a doctor. While that path didn’t unfold as planned, it led me to something unexpected: a genuine interest in coding. Encouraged by my brother, I started learning about different programming languages — and quickly realized I enjoyed solving problems and building things with code.


I'm currently deepening my knowledge in computer science with the goal of entering Middlesex University to study their Computer Science course and become an AI engineer. I've taken the initiative to teach myself Python, SQL, and HTML & CSS, and I enjoy exploring how these tools can be used to solve real-world problems through technology.


I love the challenge of coding and the satisfaction that comes from creating something functional and useful. Whether it’s building a simple calculator or analyzing data with SQL, I find joy in the process of learning and improving my skills.


When I’m not coding, you can find me reading about the latest tech trends, experimenting with new programming languages, or enjoying a good book. I’m excited to continue this journey and see where it takes me next!
